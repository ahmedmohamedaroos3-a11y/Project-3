What are Solar products, and what are they used for?

Solar products capture sunlight and convert it into usable electricity or heat, including panels, batteries, and portable chargers. They reduce reliance on the power grid, lower carbon emissions, and are built from durable materials like silicon for long-term outdoor performance.
